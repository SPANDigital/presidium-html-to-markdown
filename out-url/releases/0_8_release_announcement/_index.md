title: "ACI Kafka 0.8 Release Announcement¶"
weight: 1
---

The ACI Kafka team is excited to announce the release of ACI Kafka 0.8.
This release contains a number of new features, fixes, and improvements
in the ACI Kafka client, including a drop-in compatible Kafka API,
group.id convention enforcement, and secure delivery of client
configuration secrets. We’ve also added a new [Internal Link: Kafka
Sidecar](../dev-and-ci.html#local-environments) that can be used in
development environments for teams needing a way to integrate locally
with Kafka.

We’re also thrilled to announce the soft launch of Kafka Portal – a
new and easy way to manage your ACI Kafka Topics and Clients. We’ll be
reaching out to teams with log-in instructions as Kafka Portal
availability ramps up over the next few weeks.

For the latest documentation, including an overview of ACI Kafka and the
ACI Kafka Client, please see visit our
[External Link: documentation](https://docs.aci.apple.com/kafka).

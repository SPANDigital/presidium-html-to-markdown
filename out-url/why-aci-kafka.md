title: "Why ACI Kafka?¶"
weight: 3
---

Deploying and managing infrastructure services is hard and requires
significant investment in operations, tooling, and building knowledge of
the underlying system.

Apache Kafka is no different – managing thousands of nodes, topics, and
clients and hundreds of clusters and customers requires a re-think of
how to scale build outs, operations, multi-tenancy, and customer
support.

The ACI Kafka team builds APIs, tools, and services for managing Apache
Kafka at scale, and we’re growing our expertise as we on-board and
support different use cases and workloads. We’ve met with
infrastructure teams from around the company to learn from their
experience with operating Apache Kafka, and our goal is to use this
knowledge as a foundation for managing a service that can be easily
integrated into data pipelines and SOAs.

We also provide tools for teams to manage their Kafka resources and
handle all the responsibilities of running a managed service. Read more
about our Topics-as-a-Service and the tools we offer to manage Kafka
resources in the [Internal Link: Overview section](overview.html).

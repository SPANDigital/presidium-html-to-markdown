### What cluster does my ACI Kafka Namespace live on?[Internal Link: ¶](\#what-cluster-does-my-aci-kafka-namespace-live-on)

In general, we try to abstract the concept of a cluster away from our customers.
However, for network connectivity it is something that is more important to understand.
You can discover which cluster your namespace lives on via the [Internal Link: CLI](../self-service/kafka_cli.html#fetch-single-namespace) or [Internal Link: Public API](../self-service/public_api.html#fetch-single-namespace).

---
title: "Expansion for Existing Customers¶"
weight: 2
---

If you’ve previously on-boarded to ACI Kafka, and you have a pre-existing
_customer group_ you can request an expansion (e.g. increased topic retention,
additional namespaces, etc) by updating the questionnaire you completed for
on-boarding and then emailing
[Internal Link: aci-queue-onboarding](mailto:aci-queue-onboarding%40group.apple.com).

Please note that requests are subject to available capacity and may require the
provisioning of additional underlying hardware resources.

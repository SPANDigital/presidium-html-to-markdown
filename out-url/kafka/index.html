ACI Kafka¶

Introduction¶

ACI Kafka is a fully hosted and managed Apache Kafka service that’s secure, highly available, reliable, and easy to use.

Start with an Overview of ACI Kafka if you’d like to learn more about our service offering, existing features, project road map, and service availability. If you’re interested in learning more about on-boarding then visit our On-boarding & Expansion page. If you have any questions not answered in our docs, don’t hesitate to contact us.

Why ACI Kafka?¶

Deploying and managing infrastructure services is hard and requires significant investment in operations, tooling, and building knowledge of the underlying system.

Apache Kafka is no different – managing thousands of nodes, topics, and clients and hundreds of clusters and customers requires a re-think of how to scale build outs, operations, multi-tenancy, and customer support.

The ACI Kafka team builds APIs, tools, and services for managing Apache Kafka at scale, and we’re growing our expertise as we on-board and support different use cases and workloads. We’ve met with infrastructure teams from around the company to learn from their experience with operating Apache Kafka, and our goal is to use this knowledge as a foundation for managing a service that can be easily integrated into data pipelines and SOAs.

We also provide tools for teams to manage their Kafka resources and handle all the responsibilities of running a managed service. Read more about our Topics-as-a-Service and the tools we offer to manage Kafka resources in the Overview section.

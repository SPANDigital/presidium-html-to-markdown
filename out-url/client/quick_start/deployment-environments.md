A list of Kaffe Endpoints for IF1 and IF-PROD can be found in the [Internal Link: Kaffe
Endpoints section](kaffe.html#kaffe-endpoints).

Your ACI Kafka onboarding contact should let you know the environment
your namespaces are located. Once you’ve obtained the Kaffe endpoint
for your environment, you can use this as the value for configuring
`pie.queue.kaffe.connect`.

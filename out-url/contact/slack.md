There are two channels on [External Link: Apple Slack](https://apple.slack.com) for ACI
Kafka support.

- [External Link: help-aci-kafka](https://apple.slack.com/app_redirect?channel=CHNJKMAMU),
  for regular questions about ACI Kafka which don’t fit in Stack
  Overflow.
- [External Link: aci-kafka-prod](https://apple.slack.com/app_redirect?channel=CJ9K5SUCC),
  only for production incident reporting and related questions on ACI
  Kafka.

Feel free to post questions or support issues there to get live help.

_Note: Although we monitor these Slack channels we offer no SLA for_
_responses there. Therefore consider escalating via email, or if it fits_
_the criteria for urgent support page us._

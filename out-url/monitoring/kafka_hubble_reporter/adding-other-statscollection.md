One thing you might want to do is to add new StatsModules or
StatsCollections to publish to hubble. _HubbleProperties_ offers you two
methods to do so.

To get more information on the hubble agent please refer to the [External Link: hubble
agent
documentation](https://github.pie.apple.com/Hubble/hubble-agent-docs)

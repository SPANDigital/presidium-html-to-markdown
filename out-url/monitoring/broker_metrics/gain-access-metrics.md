---
title: "How can I gain access to my metrics?¶"
weight: 4
---

Metrics are accessible to all members of the [External Link: `kafka-metrics-users`](adir://groups/10704005) Apple Directory (AD) group.
Individual users can join this group by adding themselves to the [External Link: `aci-kafka-announce`](adir://groups/670759) group.
If you would like to have your team’s group added, please [Internal Link: contact us](../contact.html).

Joining the AD group will provide access to metrics in the [External Link: `aci-kafka` Mosaic workspace](https://aci-kafka.grafana.telemetry.g.apple.com).
However, if you want to set up your own alerting or recording rules for these metrics you will need to own a dedicated Mosaic workspace.
Please follow the [Internal Link: How do I set up alerts?](#how-do-i-set-up-alerts) section for more details.

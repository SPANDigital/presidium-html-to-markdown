---
title: "What do I have to do?¶"
weight: 3
---

If you do not have your own Mosaic dashboards or alerts using these metrics, then no action is required.

If your team has a Mosaic sharing policy that covers these metrics, then we should have reached out to you using the
email specified in your customer group. Teams should update their queries to use the new metrics. During the transition
period, we will keep sending metrics using both the new and old systems for the clusters that have sharing policies.

We believe that it should be possible to update all queries to the new metrics with zero downtime, as part of a gradual process,
and without triggering any unwanted alerts.

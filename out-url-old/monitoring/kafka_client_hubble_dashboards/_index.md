title: "Kafka Client Hubble Dashboard Templates¶"
weight: 1
---

When using the kafka-hubble-reporter, client KPIs published to Hubble
can be charted in your team’s dashboards. As a matter of convenience,
ACI Kafka provides a template for producer and consumer dashboards so
that you can quickly get started monitoring your clients.

To learn more about the KPIs included in the templates, see

: \- [KaffeClientEvents]({{%baseurl%}}/https://github.pie.apple.com/pie/kafka-tools/blob/develop/kaffe-client/src/main/java/com/apple/pie/queue/kaffe/KaffeClientEvents.java)
\- [KafkaCryptoEvents]({{%baseurl%}}/https://github.pie.apple.com/pie/kafka-tools/blob/develop/kafka-crypto/src/main/java/com/apple/pie/queue/kafka/crypto/HubbleKeys.java)
\- [KafkaProducerEvents]({{%baseurl%}}/https://docs.confluent.io/platform/current/kafka/monitoring.html#producer-metrics)
\- [KafkaConsumerEvents]({{%baseurl%}}/https://docs.confluent.io/platform/current/kafka/monitoring.html#consumer-metrics)

---
title: "How can I gain access to my metrics?¶"
weight: 4
---

Broker metrics are only shared with eligible Apple Group directories and Consumer Group IDs.
Additionally, your cluster must be reporting metrics to Mosaic and you must have an [eligible consumer group]({{%baseurl%}}/../client/lag_metrics.html#is-my-consumer-group-eligible).
Please [contact us]({{< ref "contact" >}}) with your [customer group]({{%baseurl%}}/../concepts.html#customer-group) to on-board. If you have a dedicated ACI Kafka cluster, add its name to the onboarding request.
Synthetic metrics are currently only available for dedicated clusters.

Note

We sync metrics with new resources periodically, so metrics for a new client identity or namespace will be available a few hours after creating them.

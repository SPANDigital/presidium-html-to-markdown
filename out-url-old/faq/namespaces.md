### What cluster does my ACI Kafka Namespace live on? [¶](#what-cluster-does-my-aci-kafka-namespace-live-on "Link to this heading")

In general, we try to abstract the concept of a cluster away from our customers.
However, for network connectivity it is something that is more important to understand.
You can discover which cluster your namespace lives on via the [CLI]({{%baseurl%}}/../self-service/kafka_cli.html#fetch-single-namespace) or [Public API]({{%baseurl%}}/../self-service/public_api.html#fetch-single-namespace).

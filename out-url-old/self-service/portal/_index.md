Portal (also known as Kafka UI) is gone for good. You should use another way of self-servicing: [Kubernetes\
CRDs]({{< ref "self-service/kube" >}}) (even if you’re not using Kube at all!), [Kafka CLI]({{< ref "self-service/kafka_cli" >}}) or the [public API]({{< ref "self-service/public_api" >}}).

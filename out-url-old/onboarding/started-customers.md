To on-board with ACI Kafka, send an email stating your team and use-case
details to
[aci-queue-onboarding]({{%baseurl%}}//files/mailto:aci-queue-onboarding%40group.apple.com) in
this format:

```
 Subject: (ACI Kafka On-boarding - <Team/Project Name>).
     Org: (Name of the org you are part of).
   Group: (The group/team you are part of in the org).
Use-Case: (A little about your use-case and how you plan on using ACI Kafka).

```

An on-boarding team member will get back to you with a questionnaire for
you to complete. The questionnaire collects all key details needed to
provision both your QA and Prod services. Once your questionnaire is
completed, please send an update to
[aci-queue-onboarding]({{%baseurl%}}//files/mailto:aci-queue-onboarding%40group.apple.com). You
can expect to get your QA access within a few days of filling out your
questionnaire. Provisioning Production service is dependent on the
breadth of your use case and we will need at least one month’s notice
prior to your need for Production capacity.

We set targets for the reliability of the ACI Kafka service (service level
objectives, SLOs). Our actual performance is measured (service level indicators,
SLIs) and compared with these targets. In the case where we fail to meet these
targets we commit to take action to improve (service level agreement, SLA).

Our SLIs and SLOs are visible at the [Apple SLO Dashboard](https://slo.apple.com/?name=%2FACS%2FACI%2FKafka&amp;s_perf=too-good&amp;s_perf=good&amp;s_perf=miss&amp;sort_by=name-asc).
This page offers an explanation of the SLIs that we track and how they are
derived. If you have any questions about our SLIs, SLOs or SLAs please
[contact us]({{< ref "contact" >}}).

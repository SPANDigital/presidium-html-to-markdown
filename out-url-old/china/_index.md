weight: 1

ACI Kafka have clusters deployed in China, to support the ACI and iCloud
stacks required there. These are DCs operated by GCBD, you may know this
through the project name of GoldenGate.

This page details the relevant endpoints and configurations required for
this environment, as some differ from the US deployments in Apple Owned
Data Centers (AODCs).

Please be aware:

1. You are responsible for seeking permission from [Legal](https://legal.apple.com/OSS) to use
   the client you choose depending on its license. Please familiarize yourself with the [Open Source Software Use, Contribution and Participation Guidelines](https://lgs.apple.com/tools-resources/open-source-software-contribution-and-participation).
2. Many teams are successfully using non-Java clients but the ACI Queue
   team can only offer best-efforts support of your use of them.
3. Specifically, ACI does not offer support for features such as
   [encryption/decryption]({{%baseurl%}}/security_settings.html#payload-encryption-and-decryption-settings)
   to non-JVM clients, so be aware of this limitation before choosing to use one.

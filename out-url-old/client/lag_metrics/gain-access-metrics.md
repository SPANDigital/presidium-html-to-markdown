Lag metrics are only shared with users with [eligible consumer group](#is-my-consumer-group-eligible).
Please [contact us]({{< ref "contact" >}}) with your [client identity]({{%baseurl%}}/../concepts.html#client-identities) or your [customer group]({{%baseurl%}}/../concepts.html#customer-group) if you would like to access these metrics.
